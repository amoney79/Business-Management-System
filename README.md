> 🌍 *Transforming business management through automation, analytics, and secure digital finance.*

Sintoil BMS is a **next-generation ERP** designed to integrate **accounting, inventory, purchasing, and sales operations** into one powerful system.  
Built entirely in **Java**, Sintoil empowers businesses with **real-time insights**, **multi-company management**, and **cloud-enabled reporting**.

## ✨ Vision

> *To simplify enterprise management through intelligent automation, data transparency, and global accessibility.*

Sintoil aims to become the **digital backbone** for SMEs, enabling them to compete globally with **data-driven decision-making**, **AI analytics**, and **smart automation**.

---

## 🧩 Core Modules

### 🧾 Account Management
- Flexible **Chart of Accounts**
- Multi-company & multi-currency support  
- Group/Ledger management  
- Journal, Cash & Bank books  
- Consolidated group reports  

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Unlimited stock groups & categories  
- **Godown, Batch & Expiry Management**  
- **Job Costing & Item Cost Tracking**  
- **Re-order alerts** & stock valuation  
- Multi-unit & flexible UOMs  

### 💰 Purchase & Sales Management
- Multiple billing formats  
- Price lists & discount policies  
- Sales & purchase order tracking  
- Receipt (GRN) / Delivery (DC) management  
- Invoice & payment synchronization  

### 🌐 Internet & Cloud Features
- Secure **remote access**  
- Emailing of invoices & statements  
- Browser-based **business dashboards**  
- Online license & support portal  

---

## 📈 Reports & Analytics

**Accounting Reports**
- Ledger, Cash/Bank Books, Purchase/Sales Register  
- Bills Receivable/Payable  

**Financial Reports**
- Balance Sheet, P&L Account, Trial Balance, Ratio Analysis  

**Inventory Reports**
- Stock Summary, Movement & Ageing Analysis  
- Item-wise Profitability, Batch Summary  

**Management Control**
- Cost Centres, Budgets, Forecasts, Cash Flow, Scenario Reports

## 🏗️ Architecture

| **Layer** | **Purpose** |
|------------|-------------|
| 🖥️ **Presentation** | JavaFX UI with clean, responsive interface |
| ⚙️ **Business Logic** | Java controllers & service layer for core logic |
| 💾 **Data Layer** | MySQL/SQLite database via DAO pattern |
| ☁️ **Integration** | Emailing, PDF exports, internet reporting |

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

| Component | Technology |
|------------|-------------|
| Language | **Java 17+** |
| UI | **JavaFX / Swing** |

| ORM | **JDBC / DAO Pattern** |
